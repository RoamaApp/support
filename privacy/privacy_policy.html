1) Scope

This Policy applies to the Service operated by us under the name “Roama”. Additional terms may apply for specific features, events, or promotions. If terms conflict, the feature-specific terms prevail for that feature.

2) Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account data: display name, username/handle, password (hashed), email/phone, profile photo, gender/pronouns (optional), preferred language.
  • Profile & travel preferences: home city, languages, travel interests, budget range, availability window, safety preferences.
  • Content you post: trip posts, itineraries (dates, locations, activities), photos, comments, likes, ratings, and other UGC.
  • Messages: direct messages and in-thread chat. We process these to deliver the Service and for safety moderation as described below.
  • Calendar entries (optional): if you sync or import events to build itineraries.
  • Contact lists (optional): if you choose to discover friends; we hash or otherwise process contacts as described in-app.
  • Support & surveys: information you submit to our support channels or feedback forms.

Information we collect automatically

  • Device & usage: device identifiers (e.g., IDFA/AAID subject to your settings), OS/version, app version, language, time zone, crash logs, performance data, feature usage, in-app actions.
  • Approximate location: derived from IP or device settings to show relevant trips and safety features.
  • Precise location (optional): if you enable device location to suggest nearby trips or meeting points.
  • Cookies/SDK data: pages viewed, referral/UTM, session data. See Cookies & tracking.

Sensitive information

We do not require sensitive personal data. Please avoid sharing health, biometric, financial account numbers, or government IDs in your profile or posts. If you choose to share such data, you do so at your own discretion and it will be processed as user-generated content under this Policy.

3) How we use information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service, including creating accounts, enabling posts and messaging, itinerary tools, and social discovery.
  • Suggest trips, groups, and people you may be interested in; customize feeds and search results.
  • Safety and integrity: detect spam, fraud, or harmful content; enforce our Community Guidelines and Terms of Service.
  • Communications: send transactional messages (e.g., account, safety, itinerary updates) and—with your consent where required—marketing communications. You can opt out anytime.
  • Analytics: measure feature use, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • Legal: comply with law, defend legal claims, and prevent illegal activities.
  • With your consent: for any additional purpose disclosed to you at collection.

4) How we share information

  • With other users: your display name, handle, profile, and public posts are visible according to your settings. Messages are visible to conversation participants. Trip posts and itineraries are visible per the audience you select.
  • Service providers: hosting, analytics, customer support, safety moderation, communications (email/SMS/push), and payment processors (if applicable). They are bound by contractual obligations to protect your data and use it only per our instructions.
  • Business transfers: in mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales. We will notify you of material changes.
  • Legal & safety: to comply with valid legal process or to protect rights, property, users’ safety, or the public.
  • With your direction or consent: e.g., when you use a third‑party integration.

Ad tech We currently [do / do not] sell or share personal information for cross‑context behavioral advertising. If we do, you can opt out via Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information and in app settings.

5) Social & community content

Roama enables you to publish trip posts and interact with others. Content you mark as public may be viewable by anyone, indexed by search engines, and shared by others. Please consider your privacy before posting.

  • We may use automated systems and human review to moderate content and detect safety risks (e.g., scams, harassment, dangerous meetups).
  • We may remove content or restrict accounts that violate our policies.
  • You can report users or content in-app; urgent threats should be reported to local authorities.

6) App permissions

  • Location (approximate/precise, optional): show nearby trips and meeting spots.
  • Photos/Camera (optional): upload trip photos or profile images; scanning QR codes.
  • Contacts (optional): discover friends; we may upload hashed identifiers to find matches. You can revoke at any time.
  • Calendar (optional): import/sync events to itineraries; we only read the calendars you select.
  • Notifications (optional): trip requests, messages, reminders.
  • Microphone (optional): if you use audio features (e.g., voice notes or calls), audio is processed to provide the feature.

7) Cookies & tracking

We and our partners use cookies, SDKs, and similar technologies for essential operations, analytics, and (if enabled) advertising. You can manage preferences in the app/web cookie banner and your device settings. For web, see our Cookie Notice.

8) Legal bases (GDPR/UK GDPR)

  • Contract: to provide the Service you request.
  • Legitimate interests: to keep the Service safe and useful (e.g., moderating abuse, analytics, improving features). We balance these interests against your rights.
  • Consent: for optional features (e.g., precise location, contacts sync, marketing, ad cookies). You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal obligation: to comply with applicable laws.

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards, such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses and UK International Data Transfer Addendum. Copies of relevant safeguards are available upon request where legally permitted.
